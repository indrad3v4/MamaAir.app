Why Choose Mama Air

Environmental risks like air pollution and heat stress can silently impact your well-being and your baby's development.

With Mama Air, you stay informed and protected — anytime, anywhere:

  • Air pollution aware and alerts tailored to your location
  • Personalized recommendations to reduce health risks
  • Symptom tracking with insights designed for pregnancy
  • Supportive advice, empowering you to make safe choices

Every mother deserves a safe environment — Mama Air helps make that possible.

How It Works

Mama Air combines advanced mobile technology with intelligent cloud analytics to support your pregnancy

1

Track Your Timeline & Symptoms

Not for Emergencies

The 'Mama Air' app is not intended to be used to identify emergencies or to treat emergent, serious, or life-threatening conditions. No Health Advice

The content and recommendations on the 'Mama Air' app are not designed, intended, or appropriate to replace your relationship with your healthcare professional, and should not be relied on as health advice.
